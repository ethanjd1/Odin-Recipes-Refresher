Big Sky Burger

Description

a half-pound burger made with 100% USDA Prime Ground Beef, topped with shaved prime rib, Swiss cheese, crispy onion straws, arugula, and a steakhouse sauce. It's a hearty burger for meat lovers, featuring a blend of beef and prime rib for a rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground beef (Fat % of your liking)
  • 1 Tablespoon Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Tablespoon Red Cooking Wine
  • 1 Tablespoon Minced Fresh Basil
  • 2 whole Minced Sun Dried Tomatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon Minced Shallots
  • 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 2 slices Mozzarella
  • 4 Tablespoons Pesto Mayo
  • 2 whole Hamburger Buns

FOR PESTO MAYONNAISE

  • 1/4 cups of Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Tablespoons Prepared Pesto
Steps
  1. Combine all the burger ingredients (except cheese, pesto mayo, and buns) into a bowl and form them into 2 perfect patties
  2. Refrigerate for 10 minutes before grilling on your George Foreman Grill or BBQ grill so the burger holds its form and does not fall apart
  3. Cook until the burger is well done. Place slices of cheese on top of the burger and place them under the broiler until melted
  4. Assemble your Big Sky Burger with Pesto Mayo on both sides of the bun and any other condiment you want
